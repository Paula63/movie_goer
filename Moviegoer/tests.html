Death Note

Year of release: 2006
Genre: mystery, drama,fantasy
Episodes: 37
Directed by: Araki Tetsuro
Rating: 8.67

Description:
What will you do if the Notebook falls into your hands, having written in which someone's name you condemn it to death? How will you behave when you are behind your back, suddenly, whose voice will say “I am the god of death”? Whose name will you write in this notebook with your first hand, how will you prepare him for his death?
Light, an intelligent promising young man, realizing the power and strength of this notebook, decides to create an ideal world - without violence, crime and evil. But in what ways will he go to this, is he ready for everything in order to become the god of the new world? What will he do when he has no less strong opponents on his way who disagree with his methods - the police, L, his father?
Does he own this notebook or has this notebook taken possession of him?
Whose name will be the last in the tetrad of death?
