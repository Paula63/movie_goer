Death Note

Year of release: 2006
Genre: mystery, drama,fantasy
Episodes: 37
Directed by: Araki Tetsuro
Rating: 8.67

Description:
What will you do if the Notebook falls into your hands, having written in which someone's name you condemn it to death? How will you behave when you are behind your back, suddenly, whose voice will say “I am the god of death”? Whose name will you write in this notebook with your first hand, how will you prepare him for his death?
Light, an intelligent promising young man, realizing the power and strength of this notebook, decides to create an ideal world - without violence, crime and evil. But in what ways will he go to this, is he ready for everything in order to become the god of the new world? What will he do when he has no less strong opponents on his way who disagree with his methods - the police, L, his father?
Does he own this notebook or has this notebook taken possession of him?
Whose name will be the last in the tetrad of death?
                                                                                                    



Code Geass

Year of release: 2006
Genre: Adventure, Fantasy, Fur, Drama, Comedy
Episodes: 25
Directed by: Taniguchi Goro

Description: Alternative reality: the British Empire seized a third of the world, bringing down the rebellious might of its military machine.
In Japan, captured and renamed into “Zone 11”, along with the younger sister lives high school student Lelouch, the expelled son of the Emperor, who lost his mother and the right to the throne. The young man differs from his peers not only by his origin - he is a very gifted person, only where to apply these abilities? Suddenly, Lelouch is involved in a clash between Japanese terrorists and British soldiers. So he meets the mysterious girl "C.C." She gives him the supernatural ability "Giass", allowing to subdue the will of other people.
Hiding his face in a mask, under the name "Zero" Lelouch joins the underground movement, eager to take revenge on Britain and his father. As a student at the Ashford Academy, the young man is forced to lead a double life. But it is not he alone who has to hide his face and his true goals from others ...
This series combines the best traditions of a mecha-action movie, a political detective story and a school drama. The first season is dedicated to the beginning of the struggle of Lelouch and the nascent confrontation between him and his best friend, who took the side of the Empire.
