Major [TV-6]

Year of manufacture: 2010
Genre: Sport, Drama
Episodes: 25
Directed by: Fukushima Ricky

Description:
After a dramatic match in the World Cup finals, Goro Shigeno received wide acclaim and made it to Major League Baseball - America's premier baseball league. The audience, reporters, and the owner of the Indiana Hornets Club place great hopes on a 19-year-old pitcher, but experienced players, including new teammates, are not in a hurry with the assessments - they saw the young and the early players in big sports in abundance. Goro himself, in spite of a cheerful look, is in fact in a deep crisis. The cause of his life, the rivalry with Joe Gibson Sr., is over, and now the guy is moving forward by inertia, not understanding what to strive for.

For each action, a reason is needed, after achieving some goals, you need to set others. It’s hard for a guy who is far from family and friends. The young talent has yet to realize that the strength for self-affirmation in the best world league and rivalry with the same young baseball genius Gibson Junior should be sought not in superficial bravado and arrogance, but in the depths of his own soul. Of course, understanding will come with experience - no wonder that in the East they say: “The older, the better.” Most importantly, Goro has the necessary qualities. To become a great player, you need only time. Well, the appearance of Shimizu-chan clearly does not hurt!


Do not give up!

Genre: everyday, comedy, romance, drama, junior girl
Episodes: 25
Directed by: Sayama Kiyoko

Description:
Kyoko Mogami from childhood loved the fairy tale about Cinderella. Therefore, she firmly knows that at the beginning of the fairy tale, the heroine needs to work a lot, walk messily and hope for the best - then at the end she will have fabulous outfits, a series of balls and a wedding with the prince.

The prince is already there - this is Kyoko's former classmate, handsome Shou Fuwa, who went to Tokyo to become a pop star. Kyoko went to the capital with him. While Syo makes her way into show business, she cooks, erase and works two jobs to pay for an expensive apartment. Kyoko does not grumble, because Cinderella should behave. But then she suddenly finds out that her handsome prince appreciates Kyoko only as a servant - a trouble-free, albeit rather tedious, housemaid. That he took her to the capital only for housekeeping, and Kyoko’s loyalty and tenderness are completely indifferent to him and even ridiculous.

Thus ended the “fabulous” period of Kyoko’s life. Now she understood that she would be guided by holy revenge: she would prove to the mean-spirited Syo, that his “boring maid” was a real star, and that he was the last idiot who had neglected her !!! It is said - done: Kyoko is sent to storm the agency for the promotion of young talents (of course, the one that competes with the agency of her former prince) ...


Clannad

Year of release: 2007
Genre: Romance, Comedy, Drama, School
Episodes: 22
Directed by: Ishihara Tatsuya

Description:

But everything changes when in the spring he meets Nagisa Furukawa, who has returned to school, a sweet and strange girl who loves buns, talks with herself, and also dreams of reviving the theatrical circle. Helping Nagisa, Tomoya realizes that life is not over, and his heart begins to thaw. In addition to Nagisa and his old friend, Sunohara, Tomoy’s life includes Kyo and Ryo’s sisters, strong and purposeful Tomoyo, Kotomi’s school genius, absolutely helpless in real life, and absolutely strange Foucault is a lover of starfish and school weddings.
Gradually, Tomoya notices that the lives and destinies of many people revolve around him ... and he realizes that this is not an accident. The answer lies in a strange world on the other side of dreams, in which a young girl awaits, somewhat similar to himself.

Bakuman [TV-1]

Genre: comedy, daily
Episodes: 25
Directed by: Kasai Kenichi

Description:
Good school grades - a prestigious university - a large corporation: this is the life plan of the majority of Japanese boys and girls. But in each generation there are stubborn people who are ready to reject a bird in their hands for the sake of the opportunity to preserve their individuality and do their favorite thing. Such are the young artist Moritaka Mashiro and the beginning writer Akito Takagi, who are still finishing high school, but have already made a difficult decision to devote their lives to creating manga, a unique phenomenon of Japanese culture.

The heroes of the series are fans of manga, laureates of youth contests and are familiar with the realities of the “adult” show business, where units make their way up. But when else to risk, if not in 16 years? And here, Moritaka, prone to reflection, suddenly finds out that his favorite and classmate, Miho Azuki, wants to be an actress-seiyu, that is, to work on an "adjacent specialty." Being at the mercy of euphoria, the guy immediately offers the girl two things: to play sometime in the anime in their manga and ... marry him. The most interesting thing is that Adzuki agrees to do both - but in the same strict sequence. Now the creative duo will have to put everything on the map - hard work, talent, potential career - and firmly believe in yourself and your luck. If you don’t try, you don’t know, Dragonball wasn’t built right away either!

Then she realizes that in fact he is a very lonely and vulnerable person. His best friend Masafumi Takeuuchi tells Nanami that Yano mourns the loss in his girlfriend Nana Yamomoto's car accident. The sufferings experienced by Motoharu make him even more attractive in the eyes of Nanami, and the girl impulsively confesses his love for him. To her relief and joy, the guy begins to reciprocate. He realizes that Nanami is ready to accept him as he is, despite the fact that his heart still reigns in love with the ex-girlfriend Nana, who left him shortly before her untimely death. And Nanami does not notice that Takeuchi, a faithful friend of Motoharu Yano, loves her very much ...
