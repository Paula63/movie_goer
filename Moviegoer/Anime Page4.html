Alas, at 11 years old Arima lost his mother - the only teacher and mentor - and stopped hearing his game. The pain of loss blocked his path to the piano, and Kosei seemed frozen in time, talking for two years only with childhood friends Tsubaki and Ryota. In an effort to stir up the guy, Tsubaki pulled him on a “double date” - this is where it all began!

The missing participant was the golden-haired Kaori Miyadzono, extremely impulsive and energetic. Because of her, the whole company turned out to be in a concert hall, where Kaori participated in a violin competition, instead of cinema or shopping. Kosei understood from the very first notes (he could listen to someone else's game) that in music a girl is his antipode: instead of the phenomenally exact following of the score, she plays as you please! Well, the opposites converge, and after a couple of days of dating, Miyadzono firmly took matters into her own hands and offered Arime to accompany her at the second stage of the competition. And there after all, and before returning to the scene close!

Dr. Ozaki and other locals who have not lost their courage pledge themselves to understand the secret and go to the end. Their fate will be decided by the magnificent fantasy of the author of "The Twelve Kingdoms"!
